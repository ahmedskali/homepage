Ahmed Skali

Welcome! I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Global Economics & Management at the University of Groningen.

I am also an Associate Editor & Methods Advisor at The Leadership Quarterly, as well as a fellow of the Global Labor Organization (GLO) and of the Households in Conflict Network (HiCN).
